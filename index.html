About Academic Pressure

Academic pressure is stress related to exams, deadlines, and expectations. When unmanaged, it can affect both mental health and academic performance.

Main Causes

High Expectations

Pressure from parents, teachers, and society.

Exam Anxiety

Fear of failing or underperforming.

Time Management

Balancing academics and personal life.

Effects

  • Burnout
  • Lack of sleep
  • Low motivation
  • Anxiety
